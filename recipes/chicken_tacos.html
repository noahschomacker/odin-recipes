Chicken Tacos

Perfect Chicken Tacos

There is nothing better after a long day than tacos. The best part about this recipe is most of the work can be done before you even get home!

Chicken tacos have been a staple in my family growing up, and I can't wait to bring them to you! You will be craving this every day while you are at work.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Torillas
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mexican Blend Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Limes
  1. Place chicken breast in pressure cooker with a little water. Add salt and pepper. Cook for 15 mintues.
  2. Once chicken breast is cooked, shred chicken.
  3. Place shredded chicken into pan, add taco seasoning.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. While taco meat is cooking, place sour cream in a bowl and squeeze half a lime into bowl and mix.
  6. Once taco meat is done, place meat onto tortilla.
  7. Top with lettuce, cheese, sour cream and lime sauce, salsa.
  8. Enjoy!
