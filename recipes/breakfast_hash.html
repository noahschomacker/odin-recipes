Breakfast Hash

Breakfast Sausage Hash

The perfect low calorie, filling breakfast for weight loss.

Eat this to keep yourself full all day with minimal calories and A LOT of food.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Hashbrowns
  • Sausage Links
  • 3 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Onion
  1. Dice a quarter of an onion and throw in pan on high heat with butter.
  2. Dice up 3 sausage links and throw in pan with onions.
  3. Once onions are brown, throw in shredded hashbrowns and mix with everything in pan.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Let hashbrowns cook for a few minutes and flip.
  6. Crack 3 eggs over the top of hashbrowns.
  7. Pour just a little water into sides of pan to create steam and put lid over pan.
  8. Once eggs are done, top with salt and pepper.
  9. Enjoy!
