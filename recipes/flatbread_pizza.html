Flatbread Turkey Pepperoni Pizza

Healthy Flatbread Turkey Pepperoni Pizza

The perfect light lunch for a cut. This recipe will satisfy your pizza cravings, while keeping your calories on track.

I eat this pizza recipe every single day to lose weight. I am down over 40 lbs!

Ingredients

  • Lavash Flatbread
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Turkey Pepperoni
  • Pizza sauce
  1. Preheat oven to 350. When you press start, put a Lavash Flatbread the oven on a pan while it preheats. (This will toast the crust).
  2. When flatbread turns a golden brown, take out of oven and flip.
  3. Place a few spoonfuls of pizza sauce.
  4. Place 2 servings of mozzerella cheese.
  5. Place a serving of Turkey Pepperoni.
  6. Place in oven until cheese is melted.
  7. Set oven to broil and take out as soon as cheese starts to bubble.
  8. Take out of oven, let cool off, and enjoy!
